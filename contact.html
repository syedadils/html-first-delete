How to Contact foodpanda or Get In Touch?

If you wish to contact us regarding any questions or comments you may have, please send an email to support@foodpanda.pk or connect with us via our inbuilt help centre.

You can also initiate a chat for your order during the course of delivery or after it by selecting Help Centre > My Orders > Get Help
